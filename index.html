About Me

Hey, I'm Akshay Barala, a Jaipur-based graphic designer with 1.5+ years of professional experience. I specialize in jewellery retouching, brand identity, and social media content that helps businesses stand out.

Currently working as a Photo Editor & Graphic Designer at Anirudh Jewels, I handle everything from product retouching to catalog design and social media visuals. My design philosophy is simple: create bold, clean, and effective visuals that tell a story.

I completed my BSc in 2022 and followed it with a Diploma in Graphic Design (2024) to sharpen my skills. I've also completed an internship at Third Eye, where I gained hands-on experience in creative campaigns.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring new design trends, learning new tools, or working on personal creative projects.
